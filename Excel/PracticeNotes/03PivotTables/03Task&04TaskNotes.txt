Task 03
-------
Who are our top 10 customers?
-----------------------------

Step 1: Select the Data
-------
    Select the complete dataset.
    Go to Insert → PivotTable

    Choose:
    -------
        New Worksheet or Existing Worksheet
        Click OK

Step 2: Add Fields to Pivot Table
------
In the PivotTable Fields panel:
-------------------------------
Drag Fields

| Area   | Field           |
| ------ | --------------- |
| Rows   | Customer ID     |
| Values | Purchase Amount |

Excel automatically changes:
----------------------------
    Purchase Amount → Sum of Purchase Amount

Step 3: Sort Purchase Amount in Descending Order
-------
To display highest purchase amount first:
-----------------------------------------
    1. Click any value inside Sum of Purchase Amount
    2. Right Click
    3. Choose:
    ----------
        Sort → Sort Largest to Smallest

    Now customers are sorted in descending order.

Step 4: Apply Top 10 Filter
-------
To show only the top 10 customers:
----------------------------------
    1. Click the dropdown beside Customer ID
    2. Select:
    ----------
        Value Filters → Top 10
